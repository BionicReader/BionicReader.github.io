Portfolio — 2023–2025

Evie Melhuish

Game Designer Programmer 3D / 2D Animator

I build games from the ground up — systems, code, and the art and animation that bring them to life. My solo capstone project, a psychological VR horror experience called Darker, took me from first-time Unreal Engine user to shipping a full narrative build in 13 weeks. I'm currently completing a Graduate Diploma in Digital Innovation at Victoria University of Wellington, sharpening my animation and game design theory further. I release my games under the name BIRE — short for Bionic Reader.
